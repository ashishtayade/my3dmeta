About Us

Since the birth of My3DMeta in 2016, our mission has been to provide phygital identity that allows people to celebrate important moments in the lives of people. With the advent of metaverse and we making cutting-edge progress in 3D modelling we aim to enable humans to freely express their authentic selves. We are enabling humans & pets to create their realistic and funky avatars.

My3DMeta is building an indispensable tech platform that empowers both consumers and businesses in creating super realistic, life-like and accurate next generation 3D avatars for humans & pets in one tenth of industry average time, and fraction of cost. These avatars are essential in the metaverse and NFT space for humans to express their identity fully in professional, casual or social settings as they work, collaborate, play, interact, celebrate or have fun.
