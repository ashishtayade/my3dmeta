Business App

Creating professional avatars along corporate hierarchies.

Our corporate frontend would enable the creation of rich next-gen 3D realistic and casual avatars of humans for professionals. Business customers will have corporate logins and will be able to create extensible corporate hierarchies. Through corporate login, users will be able to take or upload a 2D image of any employee to kickstart the creation of custom corporate avatars.

An employee will have one or multiple avatars. To embellish avatars, we will partner and integrate with well-known fashion and lifestyle brands to get branded formal and casual apparel accessories for humans. We can have inhouse brands of 3D fashion and lifestyle 3D objects and minted 3D NFT objects on the supply side.

Through business app, we aim to empower humans with a digital identity ecosystem to create and own their identities anywhere in the metaverse. Creators will own the full market rights for the digital assets that will be purchased and rented in the economy. With the Metaverse approaching fast, everything in the physical world will be required in the digital world. The platform will be used to provide the richest digital assets space powered by the imagination of creators in the app.
