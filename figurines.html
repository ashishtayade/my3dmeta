Figurine

Future life will be defined by Metaverse+ that constitutes of all that is Real, Persistent virtual & MR that is virtual merged with real.

Figurines at My3DMeta represent the IRL connection to Metaverse. Figurines will bridge the metaverse to the real world. Through their physical forms, figurines celebrate real moments in life which have virtual presence as NFTs or 3D human or pet avatars. We aim to continue with our Phygital journey as the NFT movement is not just a paradigm shift in technology but culture. In the metaverse+ world these NFTs and IRL connections as figurines will play a big role. We would encounter the cultural current and community projects evolving each other as we traverse in the future.

Communities will drive the digital identities that will in turn drive the culture. On the other hand the ever expansive evolving culture will impact the digital identities and communities. Individuals want to be themselves and express their own identity as individuals and communities. As we scratch the surfaces of early days of metaverse we aim to be the ecosystem of choice for the creators in both digital and their physical resemblence.. At My3DMeta we celebrate important moments in life right from birth to death. We would be the one who would like you to celebrate, commiserate or remember with us. Whether, you get an award, you have a graduation ceremony, you get together with your friends after long, important days like birthdays, weddings and anniversaries in personal and professional life, or someone loving left us, we will be there with you in the virtual and real world. And we will keep on celebrating and connecting with you in these moments all the more. With metaverse arriving, we aim to provide you with richer ways to express yourself and will enable IRL customised figurines for you to touch and feel, in the world we live in.
